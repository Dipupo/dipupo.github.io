Habeeb Dipo Olufowobi, Ph.D.

I am an embedded systems security researcher currently working on in-vehicle systems security. Presently, I'm investigating security issues arising out of the growing use of the access points to vehicular distributed systems, that a potential hacker can exploit. I aim to develop a mechanism and an intrusion detection system that can detect attacks on the in-vehicle networks (CAN) and transition the system into a fail-operational state. My research interests include automotive cyber security, IoT security, data provenance, and data analytics. My advisor is Dr. Gedare Bloom.
